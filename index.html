Livestock water pressure model

What is it

A model of the water pressure in a farm I worked in. I used EPANET model to estimate the workable range of pressure and highlight areas where the pressure is too high or low.

What I learned

  • Use of LIDAR data and mapping software to create a custom topographic map. I used QGis and Google Earth.
  • Adapting EPANET to modelling farm irrigation which is not what it's usually used for
